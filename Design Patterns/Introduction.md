🎨Design Patterns and what they actually do?

This is a question I’ve asked myself quite a few times. I remember looking it up, reading articles, watching videos… but honestly, it wasn’t as easy to understand as I expected.

At some point, I started comparing design patterns to quantum physics ⚛️. No matter how much I read, I kept feeling like I understood it—until I realized I actually didn’t. It’s one of those topics where your understanding keeps evolving, and what seems clear at first can get confusing again.

Over time, things started to click. But I noticed that just reading wasn’t enough. The real breakthrough came when I began applying these patterns in my own code—experimenting, making mistakes, and refactoring what I had written.
